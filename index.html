Why bamboo

One material, chosen on purpose.

Fast-growing

Mature in years, not decades

Bamboo reaches harvest size far faster than hardwood, keeping our supply chain steady and pricing stable even at high volume.

Lightweight

Lower freight cost per unit

Bamboo's low density means more units per container and lower landed cost — an advantage that compounds at bulk order sizes.

Renewable

An easy story to tell your customers

For eco-conscious brands, bamboo is a genuine sustainability claim, not a marketing stretch — a message that resonates at checkout.
