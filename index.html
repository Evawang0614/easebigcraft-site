Two grains, one supply chain

Why we carry both materials.

Bamboo

Fast-growing, lightweight, renewable

Bamboo matures in a fraction of the time hardwood does, making it a cost-efficient and sustainable choice for high-volume kitchenware, personal care items and promotional goods.

Wood

Density, durability, a heavier feel

For products where weight and grain character matter — furniture components, home decor and premium gift items — we source hardwood and softwood from vetted regional mills.
